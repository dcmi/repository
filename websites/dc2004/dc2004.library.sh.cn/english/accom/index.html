HILTON SHANGHAI ★★★★★

The Hilton Shanghai rises an impressive 43 stories above Shanghai's commercial district, is 20 minutes from the Hongqiao Airport, 50 minutes from the new Pudong International Airport via the highway, and 10 minutes from the Shanghai Library. It is within walking distance to a subway station, commercial buildings, shopping and tourist sites, and a 15-minute ride to some of Shanghai's best known sites - the Bund, Shanghai Museum and the Shanghai Grand Theatre.  The hotel’s 772 guestrooms and suites meet the highest standards in luxury, service and high-tech conveniences such as 2MB broadband Internet access in all rooms. Each room has a marble bathroom and rooms are equipped with a full minibar, IDD/DDD telephone with a voice mail message system and remote control television offering 24 hour satellite programs such as CNN, BBC, CNBC, Deutsche welle, Rai Int'l, HBO, Star TV, and French TV5.
